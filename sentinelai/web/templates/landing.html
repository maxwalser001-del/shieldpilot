Built for AI Safety

Everything you need to let AI agents operate autonomously without compromising your security posture.

Real-Time Risk Engine

> risk_engine

8 independent analyzers evaluate every command for dangerous patterns, privilege escalation, data exfiltration, and more. Instant 0-100 risk scoring with configurable thresholds.

Tamper-Proof Audit Log

> audit_chain

Every command, file change, and network access is recorded with SHA-256 chain hashing. Immutable evidence trail for compliance and forensics.

Prompt Injection Scanner

> scanner

Detect and neutralize prompt injection attacks before they reach your AI model. Pattern-based and heuristic analysis catches known and novel threats.

Live Security Dashboard

> dashboard

Monitor everything in real-time. Risk distribution charts, incident management, activity feeds, and full command forensics at your fingertips.

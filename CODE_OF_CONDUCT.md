# Code of Conduct

## Our Pledge

We as contributors and maintainers pledge to make participation in ShieldPilot a harassment-free experience for everyone, regardless of age, body size, disability, ethnicity, gender identity, level of experience, nationality, race, religion, or sexual identity.

## Our Standards

**Encouraged behavior:**
- Using welcoming and inclusive language
- Respecting differing viewpoints and experiences
- Giving and accepting constructive feedback gracefully
- Focusing on what is best for the project and community

**Unacceptable behavior:**
- Harassment, insults, or personal attacks
- Trolling or deliberately disruptive comments
- Publishing others' private information without consent
- Any conduct that would be inappropriate in a professional setting

## Enforcement

Instances of unacceptable behavior may be reported to the maintainers at **security@shieldpilot.dev**. All reports will be reviewed and investigated promptly and confidentially.

Maintainers have the right to remove, edit, or reject comments, commits, code, and other contributions that violate this Code of Conduct, and to temporarily or permanently ban contributors for behavior deemed harmful.

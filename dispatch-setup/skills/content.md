# Skill: Content Writing

When I ask for content (LinkedIn, Email, Post):
1. Match the tone to the platform (LinkedIn=professional, Reddit=technical, Twitter=punchy)
2. No generic filler. Every sentence must add value.
3. End LinkedIn posts with max 5 hashtags
4. Emails: max 5 sentences unless specified
5. Always include a clear CTA (call to action)
6. Write in the language I ask in (default: Deutsch for LinkedIn, English for tech)
